FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the Cantonese transcription?

Our AI achieves excellent accuracy for Cantonese mixed with English dialogue, which is commonly used by Hong Kong people, and supports Mandarin as well. The system is specifically trained to understand local terminology, business jargon, and natural code-switching between Cantonese, Mandarin, and English as typically spoken in Hong Kong business environments.

What audio/video formats are supported?

Voicebot supports all common audio and video formats including MP3, MP4, M4A, and WAV. Files can be uploaded directly through the web interface from any device on your network.

How does on-premise deployment work?

Voicebot is a web-based system that can be accessed by different devices within your intranet. It's deployed as a complete on-premise solution entirely within your organization's infrastructure. All processing happens locally - no data ever leaves your secure environment. Your team can access it from any device on your network while maintaining complete data isolation.

Can I customize the meeting minutes format?

Yes! Voicebot offers complete customization of meeting minutes templates. You can define sections, formatting styles, what to include/exclude, and specific instructions for different meeting types. Create multiple templates for board meetings, client calls, team updates, etc.

How long does processing take?

Processing is remarkably fast - typically 2-3 minutes for a half-hour meeting. The actual processing time varies depending on your hardware specifications and recording file size. Once processed, transcripts and minutes can be viewed directly in the Voicebot interface or downloaded for distribution.

Does it identify different speakers?

Yes, Voicebot automatically identifies and labels different speakers in conversations, making transcripts more accurate and organized for easy review and follow-up.

How secure is my data with Voicebot?

Your data security is our top priority. Voicebot includes automatic data cleanup within specified periods that you configure. All data remains within your secure infrastructure with on-premise deployment. You have complete control over retention policies, ensuring sensitive meeting data is automatically purged according to your organization's security requirements.
