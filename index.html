Smartgent

Smartgent is an integrated AI platform designed to streamline enterprise operations by unifying advanced conversational AI, intelligent information retrieval, and dynamic workflow automation. Available as both a SaaS and on-premise solution, Smartgent offers flexibility to meet diverse organizational needs.

Key Features

Conversational AI (LLM Chat)

Engage with a powerful large language model that understands and responds to complex queries, facilitating seamless human-computer interactions.

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

Enhance response accuracy by integrating real-time data retrieval with generative AI, ensuring informed and contextually relevant outputs.

Workflow Automation

Design and implement automated workflows that adapt to business processes, reducing manual effort and increasing efficiency.

Deployment Options

SaaS

Access Smartgent through a cloud-based service, ensuring rapid deployment, scalability, and minimal maintenance overhead.

On-Premise

Deploy Smartgent within your organization's infrastructure to maintain data sovereignty and comply with internal security protocols.

Voicebot

Key Features

Multilingual Support

Intelligent Organization
