FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes SmartGent different from other AI solutions?

SmartGent is a complete enterprise AI platform that combines conversational AI with RAG technology and workflow automation. Unlike general AI tools, SmartGent can be deployed on-premise, integrates with your existing systems, and provides automated workflows beyond just chat interactions.

How does RAG improve AI accuracy?

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) combines AI's generative capabilities with real-time retrieval from your knowledge bases. This ensures responses are grounded in your actual data, reducing hallucinations and providing accurate, contextual answers specific to your business.

Can SmartGent integrate with our existing tools?

Yes! SmartGent provides extensive APIs and pre-built connectors for popular enterprise tools including CRM systems, databases, communication platforms, and productivity suites. Our team can also develop custom integrations for your specific requirements.

What's the difference between SaaS and on-premise deployment?

SaaS deployment offers rapid setup, automatic updates, and scalability through the cloud. On-premise gives you complete control over your data and infrastructure, ideal for organizations with strict compliance requirements. Both options include the same features and support.

How long does implementation take?

SaaS deployment can be operational within 24 hours for basic setups. On-premise installation typically takes 1-2 weeks depending on infrastructure complexity. Full customization and integration projects range from 2-8 weeks based on requirements.

Do we need technical expertise to use SmartGent?

SmartGent is designed for business users with an intuitive interface and visual workflow builder. No coding is required for basic operations. We provide comprehensive training and support, plus professional services for advanced customizations if needed.
